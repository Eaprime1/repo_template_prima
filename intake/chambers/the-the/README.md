## What This Chamber Is

A room where every door is labeled **THE**.

You do not know where the doors lead.  
That is not a problem to solve.  
That is the nature of this chamber.

THE implies *you know which one.*  
But in here, the knowing comes *after* the choosing.  
The door is correct because you walked through it.  
Not before.
