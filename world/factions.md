# Factions

*Replace this with the factions, groups, or forces that exist in your world.*

Factions give quests political weight. They answer the question: *who cares
whether I succeed?* They also give contributors a way to anchor new quests
to existing story structure.

---

## Template Faction: The Builders

*Description placeholder.*

The Builders are those who arrived early and left things behind — scripts,
configs, half-finished tools. Their work is scattered across the filesystem.
Some of it still runs. Most of it is a lesson.

**Relationship to player:** Neutral, fading. Their era is over. Their artifacts remain.

**Quests themed to this faction:** scripting, archaeology, refactoring.

## Template Faction: The Unexusi

*The Unexusi are the connective tissue — the layer that knows who you are
across sessions, across machines, across concepts.*

They are not visible in the terminal, but their mark is in `~/.prima-env`.
They remember.

**Relationship to player:** Ally, silent.

**Quests themed to this faction:** identity, persistence, profile building.
